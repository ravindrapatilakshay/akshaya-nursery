About Us

Akshaya Nursery and Farms is a wholesale plant nursery based in Nerigam, Hosur, Tamil Nadu. We grow and supply high-quality plants in bulk to nurseries, landscapers, garden centres, and businesses across South India.

Our polyhouse facility ensures year-round availability and consistent quality. We specialise in ornamental and aromatic plants grown under controlled conditions for the best results.
